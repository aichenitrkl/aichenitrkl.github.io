The Dyson sphere

The Dyson sphere is a theoretical megastructure that completely facilitates the sun's energy harnessing. Popularized By Freeman Dyson in his paper in 1960, This megastructure encompasses a star and helps to harness the energy output of a star.The Sun produces 3.8 x 1026 Watts of energy every second. What if we were to harness every last drop of this energy? How will it help humanity progress as a civilization? Let us find out. So, understand this fascinating concept with our latest blog!!
